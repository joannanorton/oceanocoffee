Our vision

Welcome to Eirene, where every sip tells a story of meticulous craftsmanship and unparalleled quality. Our journey begins amidst the lush hills of Quimbaya, Colombia, where the soul of our coffee is nurtured through four distinctive steps:

Harvesting

carefully
picked beans

In the heart of Colombia's coffee-growing region, skilled hands carefully pluck the ripest cherries from the verdant trees. Our farmers, stewards of tradition and guardians of quality, select only the finest beans, ensuring each harvest embodies the essence of Colombian coffee excellence.
At Eirene Cafe, we prioritize sustainable farming practices, partnering with local communities to uphold eco-friendly methods that preserve both the environment and the rich heritage of Colombian coffee culture.
Washing
From the harvest, our precious beans embark on a journey of purification. Nestled within our state-of-the-art wet mill, they are delicately cleansed, removing any impurities while retaining their inherent flavors. Here, amidst the gentle cascades of water, the essence of Quimbaya's terroir infuses each bean with a distinct character.

Eirene Cafe's commitment to quality extends beyond the farm, as we invest in cutting-edge technologies that ensure the purity and integrity of every bean, guaranteeing a pristine cup with each pour.
Depulping
Liberation awaits within the depths of our de-pulping process. Through meticulous precision, the cherries shed their outer layers, revealing the precious seeds within. Guided by expert hands, this transformation unfolds, unveiling the raw potential that lies at the heart of every Eirene Cafe blend.

At Eirene Cafe, we celebrate the artistry of de-pulping, where tradition meets innovation. Our dedication to preserving the natural essence of Colombian coffee elevates each bean to its fullest expression, promising a symphony of flavors in every brew.
Roasting

time to warm
the beans up

In the hands of our master roasters, the green beans undergo a metamorphosis, as they are carefully roasted to perfection. Here, in the crucible of our roastery, the alchemy of heat and time transforms raw beans into a masterpiece of flavor, complexity, and balance.
